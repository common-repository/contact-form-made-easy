== Description ==

Contact Form Made Easy is the Wordpress plugin which makes it easier to integrate the contact form on your pages, also with this contact form specifically, you can categorize the mailing lists and can distribute it across the different pages of the website

Major features in Contact Form  include:
* Flexibility to generate as much contact form as you want 
* Can integrate different contact form on different pages
* Can filter the leads in the backend based  on  Categories

Powered By <a href="https://kbizsoft.com">Kbizsoft</a>
== Installation ==

Upload the Contact Form Made Easy plugin to your blog, Activate it, then under setting > Easy Contact Form you can manage it 

1, 2, 3: You're done!
